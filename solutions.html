Naturalization & Normalization

Flood management practices with a combined between naturalization & normalization approach will be more effective to do. Naturalization is useful in the dry season, because people have water stores filled in the naturalized product reservoir. Water reserves for prolonged dry seasons are also safe. While normalizing, it is more suitable for the rainy season because it makes the water volume increase. When the water can no longer be contained, normalization causes the water to go directly to the sea.

Green Open Spaces

The construction of green open spaces is intended to protect water catchment areas. Green space is expected to maintain a balance between the natural environment and the built environment which is useful & benefits to the community.

No More Reclamations

Jakarta Bay reclamation projects limit river streamlines to the sea. It will lead to garbage accumulation. Restoring the Jakarta Bay Reclamations can be done by strengthening the coastal embankments and rivers, so the risk of flooding can be minimalized.


Increasing of Water Absorpsion Area

Natural areas such as forests, river banks, lakes and reservoirs must be restored to store water and replenish the groundwater.

Decreasing of Ground Water Pump

These days water pipe infrastructure serves every residential, business and industrial needs. Non-latrine waste can be recycled, because the sediment is naturally filled with water, land subsidence will slow down. With fewer inundated areas, the risk of flooding can be minimalized.


Waste Management

By Improving waste management, it will make the river cleaner, it also leads to better quality of life.
